Patch 3806

Date: April 1st 2024

Fixes various bugs and visual inconsistencies that were reported by players.

With gratitude to all those who took the time to report issues,

Jip

Bug Fixes

  • Fix the teleport reticle crashing when using a modded scoreboard.
  • Fix the teleport reticle not working on maps not aligned to the top left corner
  • Fix a bug where the Seraphim Air Factories are more sensitive to the terrain than the air factories of other factions.
  • Fix a bug where the build icons of the build queues of units would randomly pop up on screen when observing and/or watching a replay.

Balance

With the solid argumentation of Derp we decided to revert the revert of the revert that reverts the revert of the Tele Billy.

Other

Attempt to fix odd behavior with the ACU when it gains veterancy.

With thanks to the community and specifically to Deribus for investigating.
